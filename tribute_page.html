Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. As co-founder and CEO of Apple Computers, Steve brought us the first personal computer, the ipod and the iphone.

    Time Line of Steve Job's Life

  • 1976 - Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniack co found Apple
  • 1984 - Presents the 128k Macintosh Computer
  • 1985 - Fired from Apple
  • 1986 - Founded Pixar
  • 1997 - Returns to Apple
  • 2000 - Steve Jobs becomes Apple CEO
  • 2001 - Steve Jobs unveils iPod at a small media event on the company's campus.
  • 2007 - Steve Jobs introduces iPhone and its revolutionary touch-screen interface.
  • 2010 - Steve Jobs unveils iPad.
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs dies at home, surrounded by his family.
